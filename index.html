Who we are

Wilco Aircraft Charter

Who are we? The name WILCO Aircraft Charters is new but the certificate brings 13 years of business experience, over 100,000 hours of flight time and is Argus Platinum Rated. We are a:

  • Part 135 Certified Air Carrier -
    Certificate 6BXA771M, Wilco Aviation, LLC.
  • DOT Commuter/Scheduled Authority
  • 10 or More Authority
  • Worldwide Charter Capability

The WILCO Offering:

We offer highly focused aircraft management, maintenance and third-party charter. Our operational capabilities spread nationwide.

The WILCO People:

The Wilco Team has deep Part 135 and Part 121 charter and scheduled operations experience. Our skillset encompasses flight and maintenance with solid administration, finance/accounting, business strategy and sales. With this holistic capability, you can be confident we have what it takes to keep your aircraft available when you need it, your costs in line and the ability to maximize charter revenue to offset costs if you choose.

The WILCO Promise:

Wilco is the right size to take care of every detail important to you. Our Team will sit down with you to listen and learn what is important and then structure your program around those needs. Your guests, program, aircraft and budget will be treated like our own.

Decades of Experience

At Wilco Aircraft Charter, our team of dedicated professionals strive to provide a long lasting and unique experience that you will not find anywhere. We will make each and every flight as unique as our customers. Our concierge specialists will ensure that every detail is taken of, from the moment you call our team, during your flight and finally, when you arrive at your final destination.

Our team of professionals have culled their craft over the years, from aircraft maintenance to brokerage. No detail is too small and we will ensure you can sit back and enjoy your flight, maintaining the highest standards in all aspects. From aircraft sanitizing to qualified pilots, you will be our first priority.

We would be thrilled to work with you in whatever capacity you require and welcome an introductory call to learn more about the WILCO Experience.
